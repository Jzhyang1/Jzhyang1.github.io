About Me

I am pursuing a BS in Computer Science as a Turing Scholar at the University of Texas at Austin.

My academic interests are centered around Compilers and Concurrency. I am expanding Monomer (my programming language) to natively support concurrency.

I hope to make meaningful contributions in the software development cycle.

Outside of academics, I have been playing the piano for 13 years. My favorite composition is Beethoveen’s Appassionata (though my favorite composer is Liszt). I also draw. I try to post my more recent drawings on this site.
